Land Acquisition for Build‑to‑Rent Communities

Midwest & Sunbelt growth corridors • Class A attainable housing

Zero to Plan (ZTP) is actively acquiring land for clients developing high‑quality build‑to‑rent (BTR) communities. We focus on high‑growth corridors in the Midwest (KS, MO, IL, IA, IN) and the Sunbelt (FL, GA, SC, NC, TN). Our clients are developing 2,000+ rental units, leveraging strong regional fundamentals and robust demand. We design for community, walkability, and a pedestrian‑friendly experience, bringing human‑scale spaces to car‑dependent cities.

Our Land Target Criteria

Size & Density

  • Ideal acreage: 10–50 usable acres
  • Potential units: 200–350 (per phase)
  • Density: 4–25 units/acre

Product & Construction

  • Product: Multi‑family build‑for‑rent
  • Horizontal MF: duplex / fourplex / sixplex
  • Vertical MF: garden‑style apartments

Market Targets

  • Sunbelt rents: $1,750+ (1BR), $2,000+ (2BR), $2,200+ (3BR)
  • Midwest rents: $1,200+ (1BR), $1,500+ (2BR), $1,800+ (3BR)
  • Home values: Sunbelt $300k+ • Midwest $250k+

What Makes a Good Site?

  • Utilities available to site
  • Easy ingress/egress
  • Population & job growth
  • Highly ranked school district
  • ≤ 15‑min commute to major employment hubs
  • Proximity to amenities & neighborhood centers

Dealing with Project Opposition

  • Neighborhood charrettes to understand local needs
  • Proactive community‑outreach programming
  • Include political decision‑makers in all initiatives
  • Work with landowners & authorities to secure entitlements
