Agent Responsibilities, Program Terms & Conditions
  • Prospect Registration: For each client, you must submit a Registration Request with the client’s full name, landholding entity, and property address. Commission is payable only for a Registered Prospect.
  • Payout Mechanics: All Commission payments are made solely to your licensed brokerage firm within thirty (30) days after the trigger event.
  • Non‑Exclusivity: This agreement is non‑exclusive; Zero To Plan may source opportunities directly.
  • No Authority: You have no authority to bind Zero To Plan or make any offers on our behalf.
