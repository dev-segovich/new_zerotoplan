Zero To Plan – Broker Partnership Program

Earn 4% Commission on Land Introductions

Zero To Plan partners with landowners through a 3‑phase development structure. As a broker, you can introduce us to landowners/clients and earn 4% total commission based on the feasibility‑determined land value.

How It Works
Phases & Commission Triggers
Phase I – LOI
Zero To Plan analyzes property during a 90‑day LOI. Feasibility Value set at end of LOI.
Phase II – Development Services Agreement (DSA)
If project proceeds as JV, DSA executed. Broker earns 1% commission at DSA signing.
Phase III A – Joint Venture / Operating Agreement (JV/OA)
Final JV executed. Broker earns 3% commission at JV signing.
Phase III B – Purchase & Sale Agreement (PSA)
If landowner opts for sale instead of JV. Broker earns full 4% commission at PSA closing (purchase price).
Why Partner With Zero To Plan
  • Timely Payouts – Commission split across milestones, 1% as early as Phase II.
  • Protected Introductions – Registered prospects locked for 12 months.
  • Clear Terms – One commission per deal, no double payments.
  • Serious Backing – We handle feasibility, entitlement, and capital stack.
Broker Responsibilities
  • Submit Prospect Registration (owner name, landholding entity, property address).
  • Registration valid for 180 days; renewable.
  • First complete registration accepted = broker of record.
